Not using some accounts anymore?

If part of your old account base is no longer being used, we can review whether it makes sense for us to make an offer. The process is simple and handled directly.

How it works

Clear, simple, and direct.

The idea is straightforward: you send basic details, we review them, and if relevant, we discuss a possible buyout offer.

1

Share the basics

Send the approximate spend history, the country of the account, and whether it is active, inactive, or no longer needed.

2

We review it

We look at the basic value factors such as spend level, account country, general condition, and overall fit.

3

We discuss an offer

If it is relevant for us, we can discuss an estimated price and possible terms. Single accounts and larger batches are both possible.
