FAQ

Common questions.

Short answers before you contact us.

Do we have to sell?

No. You can simply send the account details for review. If the offer is not suitable, there is no obligation to continue.

What details are needed first?

Usually the spend range, country, account status, and whether the account is active or inactive are enough for the first review.

Can the price be higher?

Yes. The table shows estimated starting ranges. Final pricing depends on account quality, country, status, structure, and volume.

Can you review multiple accounts?

Yes. We can discuss both single accounts and larger batches from agencies or advertising specialists.
